Privacy Policy for Giftly

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Giftly ("we", "our", or "the app") is a gift and celebration reminder app developed by Nikhil Yadav. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information. By using Giftly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Our Core Principle: Your Data Stays on Your Device

Giftly is an offline-first app with no account and no sign-up. All the information you enter — the people you add, their birthdays and anniversaries, relationships, interests, notes, budgets, gift plans, photos, and your own profile details — is stored only on your device. We do not operate a server, we do not maintain a database of users, and we cannot see, access, or collect any of this information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store on any server:

  • Your name, email, or contact details
  • The people, dates, or gifts you track
  • Photos you add
  • Any usage analytics, location data, or advertising identifiers

Giftly contains no ads, no trackers, and no third-party analytics.

3. The One Time Data Leaves Your Device: Gift Product Search

Giftly includes an optional feature that shows real product suggestions from the web (the "From the web" section and the per-gift product search). When you use this feature, the app sends a search query to a third-party search provider (Serper.dev, which returns Google Shopping results) in order to fetch matching products.

What is sent: only the search text the app builds from a person's interests, occasion, and budget range (for example, "ceramics birthday gift under ₹1,600"), or a gift's name and price cap (for example, "water bottle under ₹200").

What is NOT sent: the person's name, your name, contacts, photos, notes, or any information that identifies you or them.

Search results are cached on your device so the feature works offline and to minimize network requests. If you never use this feature, or if you have no internet connection, no data ever leaves your device.

You can review Serper.dev's privacy practices at https://serper.dev/privacy.

4. Notifications

Giftly schedules local reminder notifications on your device (for example, before a birthday). These are generated and delivered entirely on your device and are not sent through any server.

5. Permissions We Use

  • Internet — only for the optional product search described above.
  • Notifications & Alarms/Reminders — to deliver your celebration reminders on time.
  • Vibration — for gentle haptic feedback.
  • Photos / Files (via the system picker) — only to let you attach a photo you choose; the app does not scan or upload your gallery.

6. Sharing Features (Backup & PDF)

Giftly lets you export your data as a backup file (JSON) or a shareable gift list (PDF). These are created on your device, and you choose whether and where to share them using your phone's own share sheet (for example, WhatsApp or your file storage). We are not involved in and cannot see what you share.

7. Children's Privacy

Giftly does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
